Users of HTML are sometimes surprised to find that HTML gives them little control over the way that a page is displayed.
It should be remembered that HTML was developed as a means of markingup the structure of a document not as a way of determining its presentation.

Formatting text to apperar on a Web page is therefore different from formatting text to appear
