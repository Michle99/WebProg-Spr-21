How to build a Webpage for a school

Having a uniform web design is the way to go in order to ensure that user-friendly access and interaction. This prevents each instructor from having different styles from the styles used on the university homepage or clashing styles to the university preferred styles. When the school has quality control over the web design, it ensures easy navigation, interaction and access for the students.


The tags that will be used are as follows:

  • table, th,tr,td to create the table, heading for each column of the table, the rows for the table, and the contents for each rows & columns for the table respectively. Therefore, the assignments due, the dates of the exams, timetables, subjects taught, syllabuses, and teacher can be inserted in the table.

  • ul and li will make for a easy nav bar, the styling for that will depend where the placement of the nav bar is, standard webpages today have the nav bar in a dropdown menu on the side.

  • h1 - h6 to denote different sections of a page. These can be stlyed with color, font attributes for the particular course by the instructor's preference.

  • The content of the pages will be in a p tag and can be styled with background-color, font, text-align, position, margin, and padding attributes.
